About Sudan University

Sudan University of Science and Technology is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in Sudan. It was established in 1902 as the Khartoum Technical School and the School of Commerce, later evolving through several institutions into a modern university. In 1990, it officially became known as Sudan University of Science and Technology.

Historical Development

Over the decades, the university expanded to include multiple colleges and institutes: School of Arts (1946), School of Agriculture (1954), and others. In 1975, all these institutes were merged into the Institute of Technological Colleges, eventually forming the modern university.
